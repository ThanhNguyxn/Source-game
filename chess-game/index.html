How to Play:

Objective: Checkmate your opponent's king

Controls: Click a piece to select it, then click a valid square to move

Piece Moves:

  • ♟️ Pawn: Forward 1 square (2 on first move), captures diagonally
  • ♜ Rook: Horizontal or vertical any distance
  • ♞ Knight: L-shape (2+1 squares)
  • ♝ Bishop: Diagonal any distance
  • ♛ Queen: Any direction any distance
  • ♚ King: Any direction 1 square

Special Moves: Castling, En Passant, Pawn Promotion
