⚫ Black Player

Pieces: 12

How to Play:

Objective: Capture all opponent's pieces or block their moves

Controls: Click a piece to select it, then click a valid square to move

Rules:

  • Regular pieces move diagonally forward one square
  • Jump over opponent's pieces to capture them
  • Multiple jumps in one turn are allowed
  • Reach the opposite end to become a King 👑
  • Kings can move diagonally in any direction
  • Capturing is mandatory when possible
