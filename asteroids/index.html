How to Play:

Objective: Destroy all asteroids without getting hit!

Controls:

  • ↑ : Thrust (move forward)
  • ← → : Rotate ship
  • Space : Fire bullets

Difficulty Levels:

  • Easy: Slow asteroids, 3 lives
  • Medium: Normal speed, 3 lives
  • Hard: Fast asteroids, 2 lives
  • Extreme: Fast + UFO enemies attack!

Tips: Large asteroids break into smaller ones!
