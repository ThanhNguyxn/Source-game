🎯 Whack-a-Mole

Select Difficulty:

Score: 0
Time: 60s
Lives: 3❤️
Difficulty: Easy

How to Play:

Objective: Click on moles before they hide!

Controls: Click/tap on moles to whack them

Difficulties:

  • Easy: Slow moles, 60 seconds
  • Medium: Normal speed, 60 seconds
  • Hard: Fast moles, 60 seconds
  • Extreme: Fast + Special moles (bomb 💣, golden 🌟)

Special Moles:

  • 💣 Bomb: Avoid! Lose 1 life and -10 points
  • 🌟 Golden: Bonus! +20 points
