How to Play

  • Setup: Drag and drop your ships onto the left board. Click "Rotate Ship" or press 'R' to change orientation.
  • Battle: Click on the enemy board (right) to fire a shot.
  • Objective: Sink all 5 enemy ships before they destroy yours!
  • Ships: Carrier (5), Battleship (4), Cruiser (3), Submarine (3), Destroyer (2).
