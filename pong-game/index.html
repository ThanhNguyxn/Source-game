Player 1: W / S or Arrow Up / Down

Space: Start / Pause

How to Play:

Objective: Score 5 points to win!

Controls:

  • Player 1: W/S or ↑/↓ arrow keys
  • Player 2 (if PvP): ↑/↓ arrow keys
  • Space: Start/Pause game

Rules:

  • Hit the ball with your paddle
  • Ball speeds up with each hit
  • First to 5 points wins!
