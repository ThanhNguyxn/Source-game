How to Play

  • Goal: Have the majority of discs on the board at the end of the game.
  • Move: Place a disc to flank opponent's discs between two of yours.
  • Capture: Flanked discs are flipped to your color.
  • Valid Move: You must capture at least one opponent disc to make a move.
