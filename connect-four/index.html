How to Play:

Objective: Connect four discs vertically, horizontally, or diagonally

Controls: Click a column to drop your disc

Rules:

  • Players take turns dropping discs into columns
  • Discs fall to the lowest available position
  • First to connect 4 wins!
  • Game is a draw if board fills up
